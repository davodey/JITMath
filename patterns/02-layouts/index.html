Problem Step Four

▼
The 28 oz. can is cheaper per unit.
The 14.5 oz can is cheaper per unit.
They both are equal.
The 28 oz. can is more expensive.

Problem Step One

▼
Convert to unit price equation: $1.99/14.5 and $3.59/28
Convert to unit price equation: $1.99/28 and $3.59/14.5
Convert to unit price equation: $1.99 x 14.5 and $3.59 x 28
Convert to unit price equation: $1.99/$3.59

Problem Step Three

▼
Compare: 13.7 < 12.8
Compare: 13.7 = 12.8
Compare: 1.37 < 12.8
Compare: 13.7 > 12.8

Problem Step Two

▼
Divide: $1.99/14.5 = $1.37; $3.59/28 = $1.28
Divide: $1.99/14.5 = 13.7 cents; $3.59/28 = 12.8 cents
Divide: $1.99/$3.59 = 0.55
Multiply: $1.99 x 14.5 = 28.855; $3.59 x 28 = 100.52
